The Cell Division:

Baesa Bulldogs take a bite, Clipping Kalsay Phoenix wings.

In the Caloocan Association of Public Secondary Schools Athletic Association (CAPSSAA) Mens Volleyball meet, the Baesa High Schook Bulldogs and Caloocan City Science High School Phoenix faceed off twice like two storms colliding. On September 17, 2025, the Phoenix broke a 27-year drought with a 21 victory, their wings finally catching the wind again.

However, fate was cruel. A day later in the quarterfinals, the Bulldogs returned with a renewed determination. With captain Fernando Tatel IV's powerful attacks and Roey Recio's excellent blocks gave the Phoenix the upper hand. Nevertheless, Baesa made their errors into chances, taking the opening set 2522 after a tense exchange.

The Bulldogs tightened their control in the second set. The crowd was enthralled with the Phoenix aces and blocks, but the defense of the Baesa held firm, pulling away 25-17 to secure the match. The Bulldogs then moved on to the playoffs, while the Phoenix proudly concluded their run.

It was both a victory and a heartache for Kalsay. The victory from the last game proves that Phoenix could rise again; todays defeat showed that the ascent is still ongoing. In addition, their story is still one of the fire yearning to blaze brighter rather than ashes.
